Netflix Growth Analysis

Content (Movies and TV Shows) Growth

Background

Netflix mainly has two types of content, specifically movies and TV shows. The chart documents the number of content (i.e., movies, TV shows or both) were added to the platform in a given year, on a worldwide scale.

Slide Messaging

All three groupings (“MOVIE + TV SHOW”, “MOVIE”, “TV SHOW”) have an elbow point at 2015, marking the beginning of Netflix’s high growth in terms of content. This elbow point at 2015 aligns with the elbow point of revenue seen on the previous slide. This suggests that revenue and the amount of content added are potentially correlated. Also note that the amount of “MOVIE” content added experiences a plateau in 2018. The “MOVIE + TV SHOW” and “TV SHOW” didn’t exhibit a plateau in 2018 nor did revenue in the previous chart. Thus, this suggests that revenue is more so correlated with amount of total content (movies and TV shows combined) rather than in singularity movies.
